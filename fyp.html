Final Year Project


Background


As part of my degree, we were asked to complete a final year project. This weighted very heavily for both the year and the whole degree, and mine took the form of a software development project. We were expected to treat the project as a start to end project, and the final grade was given dependent on a presentation, a 13,000 word final essay, and the product itself.


Summary


The title of my project was Competitive AI in the Context of a Strategic, Non-linear Adventure Game The aim of the project was to design an adventure game that more closely mimics reality. It was possible to take multiple routes to reach several different endings within a single play, and the player could unlock and lock these paths as they made choices through the game. There were also ways that the player could lose the game, adding an element of risk to an otherwise linear adventure plot.

Screenshots


The game played out as a visual novel game, relying on text and simple images to drive the story forward. I created everything in the game; the artwork, the UI, the backend AI, even the way the text was displayed on screen has to be designed from scratch. The game was written using Python, and the elements were all drawn using Krita and Paint Tool SAI.

Release of Information
