Warwick University


About


Warwick University is a young publicly funded research university located in Coventry, England.


I started studying at Warwick in October 2012 for a Masters in Computer Science. In the summer of 2015, I did an internship in IT Services as part of Warwick's Undergraduate Internship Program. After my internship, I stayed on part time as a Junior UX until September 2016. During my time at Warwick, I learned many transferable skills.


Wireframing


Carrying forward the skills I learned during my internship, I was responsible for creating wireframes for UI changes. On these projects, I was tasked with coming up with and justifying design changes, with reference to both customer enquiries and usability intuition.

Programs used: Axure, Photoshop, Balsamiq
